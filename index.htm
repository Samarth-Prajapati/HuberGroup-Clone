Our philosophy in a few words Get to know us

Who are we? And which principles guide our day-to-day work? By sharing our mission and company values with you, we want to show you what makes us tick. Our mission We talk to our customers and work together to find solutions. Our family-like company culture is a real strength and shapes our ideas and actions. We contribute to the success of our customers because we achieve more than expected from a printing inks and chemicals manufacturer. Our company values Connected “Being connected” – to us that means taking a genuine interest in our customer’s situation and being easily available for them at any time. hubergroup employees around the world work together closely to find the best possible solution for our customers. Through this international cooperation, we have also learnt to value intercultural differences and to integrate them positively into our work. Courageous Going one step further to develop new solutions and services is in our DNA. We act with self-confidence and make courageous but well-thought-out decisions. We push past our limits because that is the only way that we can continue to develop market innovations. Curious & Creative Using our creativity to create unique ideas – that defines us. It is only by taking new approaches that we are able to develop unique solutions for our customers and, at the same time, tap into new fields of business. Even if not everything works right away, we are not afraid to make mistakes, instead we look forward to finding out each time where these ideas will take us.

Fairs & Events

ZLV Verpackungssympsioum 2022

Highly topical lectures, parallel to the exhibition, will provide plenty of material on both days, especially for the interdisciplinary exchange of ideas, and there will be plenty of opportunity for individual technical discussions and networking.

Screen Tex India 2022

SCREENTX India 2022 will take place in New Delhi and acts as a technology platform that will bridge the gap between innovation and application by bringing together international suppliers and domestic buyers. The Indian Screen-Printing Industry is multicoloured and multi-layered. In the past five decades, it has touched almost all segments such as textile, ceramic, industrial, CDs, POPs, advertising and digital.

15th PRINTPACK INDIA 2022

PRINTPACK INDIA will take place at India Expo Centre & Mart in Greater Noida, India. The event will showcase products like In-Press, Post-Press, Finishing, Converting, Digital & Signage and Packaging Machines. It also includes other areas such as Packaging Materials, Printing & Publishing, Paper and Paper Products, Logistics & Transportation Industries and allied Products.

ExpoPrint & ConverExpo 2022

The ExpoPrint & ConverExpo is the largest and most important event for graphic arts in Latin America. Every four years, it showcases state-of-the-art technologies in pre-press, printing, packaging, and finishing. It is a global reference event, whether by number of visitors or the volume of business generated in the days it takes place.

Consulting & Support

You need advice on which resins, film adhesives, pigments or additives are best suited for your products? Or which legal requirements you have to consider in the field of food packaging? Do you want to make your chemical processes more efficient or do you perhaps need support in increasing product safety? We at the hubergroup are interested in your situation. Instead of providing you with standard answers, we provide all customers with a personal contact person. If required, we are happy to offer you intensive on-site support.

Analytical laboratory

No matter whether qualitative or quantitative analyses are required - our laboratory team will get to the bottom of the substance. We draw on the broad field of analytical and chemical methods to make your product safer and more sustainable. Thanks to the close cooperation of our experts in analysis and product safety, we meet the high purity requirements of the packaging sector with food contact.

Trainings

Our employees are experienced professionals in their field. Part of our service is that we share our knowledge and support our customers in their individual challenges. With us, you can be sure: We have experts in every field. One thing is always at the forefront of our extensive range of training courses: we always focus on the needs of our customers and their employees when designing the content. Because only if the needs and requirements are precisely defined in advance can the training courses be truly successful and sustainable.

Taner Bicer

President Chemicals Division

Taner Bicer has been a member of the hubergroup management board since 1st January 2018 and from July 2020, he is heading up the Chemicals Division. Dr Dirk Aulbert
